About Our College

A B Na'ibi College of Nursing Sciences, located in Lau, Taraba State, Jalingo, Nigeria, is an esteemed nursing institution dedicated to nurturing compassionate and skilled healthcare professionals.

Our college prides itself on integrating values with rigorous academic and practical training in the nursing sciences. We are committed to producing nurses who excel in their field while upholding the highest standards of ethical practice and compassion, guided by the best of principles. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty provide a conducive learning environment that prepares our students to meet the healthcare needs of diverse communities both locally and globally.

Our Programmes
BASIC NURSING
Learn more

It is a 3-year (6 semesters) programme that is designed to train students on practical nursing skills and provide them with the required knowledge and clinical experience to become certified professionals who can practice independently and interdependently.

BASIC MIDWIFERY
Learn more

This is a 3-year (6 semesters) Basic Midwifery programme, approved by the Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria (NMCN). Designed to train competent and skilled midwifery professionals who are ready to practice in any community for the purpose of improving maternal health and reducing pregnancy related complications.

Why Choose A B Na'ibi College of Nursing Sciences?

A B Na'ibi College of Nursing Sciences stands out as a premier institution for aspiring nurses due to its unique blend of academic excellence and ethical values. Our fully accredited programs ensure that students receive top-tier education and training, preparing them for successful careers in healthcare. The college offers flexible scheduling to meet the diverse needs of our students, making it easier to balance studies with personal commitments. Additionally, our commitment to holistic development is reflected in our support for athletic activities, promoting physical wellness alongside academic growth.

With experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive learning environment, A B Na'ibi College of Nursing Sciences is the ideal choice for those seeking a comprehensive and values-driven nursing education.
